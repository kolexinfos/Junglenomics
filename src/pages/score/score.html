THERE ARE 6 TYPES OF ANIMAL CARDS (ANT,BUMBLE BEE, CHEETAH, BUTTERFLY, WASP & ELEPHANT). ALL ANIMAL CARDS CAN BE COMBINED USING ONE SINGLE INNOVATION CARD. HAVING SAID THAT, THE WASP CARD ACTS AS A PENALTY CARD, AS IT DIVIDES A PLAYERS TOTAL SCORE BY HALF (1/2) ONCE IT IS COMBINED WITH OTHER ANIMAL CARDS. THE INNOVATION CARD PROVIDES A X3 BONUS TO THE TOTAL SCORE OF ANIMAL CARDS COMBINED. ALSO, ONCE A PLAYER USES MORE THAN 1 ANIMAL CARD, HE NOT ONLY GETS TO BOOST WITH AN INNOVATION CARD BUT ALSO , HE/SHE GETS A BONUS OF 20 EXTRA POINTS PER NEW ANIMAL CARD USED.
